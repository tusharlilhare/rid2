HyperText ka matlab hai aisa text ya information jo links ke through interconnected hota hai. Yani, HyperText ka istemal aise text ke liye hota hai jisse aap kisi dusre document ya page par click karke asani se navigate kar sakte hain.


HyperText ke Features:


Links: HyperText me links (jine hum hyperlinks bhi kehte hain) hoti hain, jo kisi dusre page ya resource ko refer karti hain. Jab aap in links par click karte hain, to aapko us link se judi hui information ya page par le jaaya jaata hai.


Interactivity: HyperText user ko ek interactive experience deta hai. Iska matlab hai ki aap sirf text padhne tak simit nahi hote, balki aap dusre pages, images, ya resources tak bhi ja sakte hain.


Organized Information: HyperText ka istemal information ko structured aur organized tarike se present karne ke liye hota hai. Yeh aapko ek page se dusre page par behtar tarike se navigate karne ki suvidha deta hai.
